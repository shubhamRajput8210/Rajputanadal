About Rajputana Dal

Founded on the principles of Swabhiman, Seva, and Suraksha

Our Mission

To champion the rights and interests of the Rajput community while working towards an inclusive, prosperous India. We stand for cultural preservation, educational empowerment, economic self-reliance, and political representation based on merit and integrity.

Our History

Established in 2012 by visionary leaders from across Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh, the Rajput Janta Party emerged from a historic convention in Jaipur. Born from the need to address community-specific challenges while contributing to nation-building, we have grown into a powerful voice for social justice and development as Rajputana Dal.

Rajputana Dal Manifesto

A comprehensive roadmap for community upliftment and national progress

Education & Heritage

Establish Rajput Cultural Centers, scholarships for meritorious students, and preservation of historical monuments. Mandate Rajput history in school curriculum.

Employment & Enterprise

Create 1 lakh jobs through MSME support, startup incubators for youth, and reservation review for economically weaker sections without creamy layer.

Agriculture & Rural Development

Modern irrigation projects, MSP guarantee, farmer producer organizations, and agro-processing units in Rajput-majority districts.

Security & Justice

Zero tolerance for crimes against women, fast-track courts for atrocity cases, and enhanced representation in police and defense forces.

Housing & Infrastructure

Affordable housing schemes, rural road connectivity, and smart village programs with digital infrastructure in historical regions.

Healthcare for All

Primary health centers in every tehsil, mobile medical units, and health insurance coverage for families below poverty line.
